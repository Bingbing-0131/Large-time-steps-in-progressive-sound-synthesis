Large Time Steps in Progressive Sound Synthesis

Yuchen Su, Eris Zhang, Kangrui Xue, Doug James
Department of Computer Science, Stanford University Tsinghua University

Symplectic Euler

These are animations and spectra generated with the Symplectic Euler method and rendered with waveBlender.

Listeners are positioned differently in each video.

Simulation Parameters:

  • Young’s Modulus: \( 1.0 \times 10^{10} \,\text{Pa} \)
  • Time Step (\( \Delta t \)): \( 2.26 \times 10^{-6} \,\text{s} \)
  • Plate Thickness: \( 1.0 \times 10^{-3} \,\text{m} \)
  • Poisson’s Ratio (\( \nu \)): 0.33
  • Mesh Resolution: 1000

Implicit Euler

These are animations and spectra generated with the Implicit Euler method, which introduces strong numerical damping.

Membrane Energy Reduction
